About Us

Community Media Trust is a not-for-profit company that specialises in communication in the fields of health, human rights and gender based violence. We produce media across all platforms and are skilled in interpersonal communication and training. CMT aims to inform and inspire. We believe in promoting transparency, accountability and democracy in all areas affecting quality of life of communities.
